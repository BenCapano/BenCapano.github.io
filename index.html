The Legend of Zelda (Remake)

This was a partner project where we remade the first dungeon, the Eagle, in the original NES Legend of Zelda. We also made a custom dungeon with a unique weapon that can be accessed by pressing the "4" key.

I developed most of the weapons, worked with the audio components, and implemented some of the unique rooms.

Puzzle Ice Battle

This was a solo project that was open ended, so I decided to make a 2 player puzzle game. The players work cooperatively to defeat enemies and progress through levels, while also competing to see who can get the higher score.

As it was a solo project, I completed all the work, with some help from the links in credits.txt file.

Carrots in Space

This was a group project that was also open ended, and my group of 5 decided to make a 2 player puzzle game. The players work cooperatively to feed the space rabbits their carrots to prevent them from invading Earth to take the carrots themselves. The players have to move through worlds while encountering different enemies to ensure all the rabbits get fed.

I worked mainly on mechanics and game objects, including the lasers and their interaction with the carrots, as well as the gravity obstacles and the shooting ship enemy. I also worked on some of the UI, including working on the level and world select views. Other help we got can be found in the credits.txt file in the downloadable zip folder.
